Abstract
KILT is a protocol for self-sovereign data and interoperability built on top of the permissionless KILT blockchain. The core component of KILT is a digital identity protocol for 1. generating and managing decentralized identifiers (DIDs) , and 2. issuing and presenting digital verifiable credentials (VCs) . In contrast to other centralized alternatives, KILT features self-sovereign data as well as revocable credentials anchored to the KILT blockchain.
